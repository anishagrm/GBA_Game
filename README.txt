project summary:
1. you will first have a welcome screen, press return/enter to play the game
2. now you are in the play screen 
3. use the up/right/down/left arrow keys to navigate around the land to get your
   player to the strawberry, so it can have dinner!
4. if you get to the strawberry before the 15 second timer ends, you will see a win screen. 
   if you don't get to the strawberry in time, you will see a lose screen.
5. you can press backspace whenever to go back to the welcome screen
